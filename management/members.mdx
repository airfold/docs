## Add a Member

Navigate to "Settings" > "Members":

<p align="center">
  <img src="/images/members_bar.png" alt="data format options" width="300"/>
</p>

Enter the email of the member you wish to invite:

![member](/images/add_member.png)

Upon accepting the invite, this member will now have access to access your workspace.

## Remove a Member

To remove a member, simply click on the remove icon to the right:

![member](/images/remove_member.png)
